OWNER: GitDigital Products

AUTHOR: RickCreator87

Purpose

Defines tiers of membership, each with different contribution levels and privileges.

Definitions

· Membership Tier: A level that determines voting weight or access to member‑only forums.

Roles & Responsibilities

· GitDigital Products – Sets tier criteria.
· RickCreator87 – Tracks tier compliance.

Requirements

· Tiers are based on sustained contribution (code, funding, governance).
· Tiers are reviewed annually.

Tier Requirement Privileges
Observer Signed CLA + one contribution Read‑only mailing list
Contributor 5 merged pull requests or equivalent Can nominate for maintainer
Core Member Maintainer or sponsor (any tier) Voting on non‑technical governance
Founding Core GitDigital Products and RickCreator87 Veto over charter changes

Governance Alignment

Membership tiers are enacted by the governance process.
