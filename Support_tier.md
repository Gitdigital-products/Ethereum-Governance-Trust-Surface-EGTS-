# Support Tier

## Purpose
This document details the specific levels of recurring financial support available to individuals and small organizations who wish to contribute to ApexOS without entering a full sponsorship agreement.

## Definitions
- **Monthly Recurring Contribution (MRC)**: The amount billed every 30 days.
- **Tier Lock‑in**: Supporters may change tier at any time; benefits adjust within 48 hours.
- **Cumulative Support**: Total contributed over lifetime, used for recognition but not for tier advancement (tiers are monthly).

## Tiers (if applicable)
| Tier Name       | Monthly Range | Annual Equivalent |
|----------------|---------------|-------------------|
| Bronze Supporter | $5 – $49      | $60 – $588        |
| Silver Supporter | $50 – $199    | $600 – $2,388     |
| Gold Supporter   | $200 – $999   | $2,400 – $11,988  |

## Benefits
- Bronze: Listed in SUPPORTERS.md, special role in Discord.
- Silver: All Bronze + access to `#supporters-lounge` channel, early access to development builds (when available).
- Gold: All Silver + one personalized thank‑you video per year from Core Team, name in release notes (opt‑in).

## Requirements
- Supporters must provide a valid email address for benefit delivery.
- For Gold tier, supporters must agree to a simple code of conduct acknowledgment.
- Supporters may not resell or transfer any benefits.

## Governance alignment
Support tiers are set by the Core Team and reviewed annually. Changes to tier pricing do not affect existing supporters for 60 days. The project’s fiscal host handles all payment processing and disputes.

## Branding rules
- Supporters may use “ApexOS Bronze/Silver/Gold Supporter” badge with a flat design (no metallic effect).
- Badge must include a link to https://apexos.org/support.
- Supporters may not claim affiliation with ApexOS beyond the badge.

## Public recognition rules
- Supporters page shows tier and join month (not full date for privacy).
- Gold supporters are listed in a separate section with a star icon.
- Supporters who downgrade tier keep previous recognition for historical months but are moved to new tier section.

## Templates (if applicable)
- Tier upgrade email notification: automated via Stripe/GitHub
- Personalized thank‑you video script template (available to Core Team only)
