# Members Tier

## Purpose
This document details the specific tiers available within the ApexOS membership subscription program, including pricing, benefits, and upgrade/downgrade rules.

## Definitions
- **Member Tier**: One of Basic, Pro, or Enterprise levels as defined in `membership_Subscription.md`.
- **Seat**: For Enterprise tier, one seat represents one individual member covered by the organization’s subscription.
- **Downgrade Policy**: Members may downgrade at the next billing cycle; benefits change immediately upon downgrade.

## Tiers (if applicable)
| Tier      | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Seats Included |
|-----------|---------------|--------------|----------------|
| Basic     | $10           | $100         | 1              |
| Pro       | $25           | $250         | 1              |
| Enterprise| $100          | $1000        | Up to 5        |

## Benefits
- **Basic**: Recognition in members list, member role on forum.
- **Pro**: Everything in Basic, plus monthly live Q&A, early access to release candidates.
- **Enterprise**: Everything in Pro, plus a dedicated Slack channel with Core Team, usage analytics dashboard for the organization.

## Requirements
- Enterprise members must verify organizational email domain.
- Members paying annually receive two months free (effectively 10 months of payment for 12 months of membership).
- No refunds for mid‑cycle cancellations; benefits stop immediately.

## Governance alignment
Member tiers are non‑transferable. The Core Team may introduce new tiers or adjust pricing with 60 days’ notice to existing members. All membership revenue is reported quarterly in the public budget.

## Branding rules
- Badges for each tier have distinct borders: Basic (gray), Pro (blue), Enterprise (gold).
- Enterprise members may not use the ApexOS name in their product names.
- Members may not claim to be “official” representatives of ApexOS.

## Public recognition rules
- Member tiers are displayed on the members page with a filter dropdown.
- Enterprise members receive a physical certificate suitable for framing.
- Pro members who refer three new members get a one‑month free extension.

## Templates (if applicable)
- Membership certificate (PDF) – generated automatically upon Enterprise signup.
- Referral tracking spreadsheet template: `/templates/referral_template.xlsx`
