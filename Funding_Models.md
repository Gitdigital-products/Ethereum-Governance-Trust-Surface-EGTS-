OWNER: GitDigital Products

AUTHOR: RickCreator87

Purpose

Describes the different funding models used or considered by EGTS (e.g., sponsorship, grants, donations).

Definitions

· Funding Model: A structured method for obtaining financial support.

Roles & Responsibilities

· GitDigital Products – Chooses which models to activate.
· RickCreator87 – Evaluates technical implications (e.g., accepting crypto donations).

Requirements

· Each active model must have documented processes.
· Models must comply with applicable laws.

Active Models

1. Direct Sponsorship (as defined in sponsorship.md)
2. Self‑funding by GitDigital Products

Considered Models (future)

· Grant applications (e.g., Ethereum Foundation)
· Crypto donations (ERC‑20)

Governance Alignment

Any new funding model requires a governance amendment per GOVERNANCE.md.
