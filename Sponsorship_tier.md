# Sponsorship Tier

## Purpose
This document details the specific characteristics, criteria, and obligations for each tier within the ApexOS sponsorship program.

## Definitions
- **Tier**: A predefined level of sponsorship with fixed contribution range and corresponding benefits.
- **Tier Upgrade**: Moving from a lower tier to a higher tier by increasing contribution mid‑cycle; benefits apply pro‑rata.
- **Tier Downgrade**: Moving to a lower tier at next renewal; benefits are reduced accordingly.

## Tiers (if applicable)
### Bronze Tier
- Contribution: $1,000 – $4,999 annually
- Maximum 1 named contact per sponsor

### Silver Tier
- Contribution: $5,000 – $14,999 annually
- Maximum 2 named contacts

### Gold Tier
- Contribution: $15,000 – $49,999 annually
- Maximum 4 named contacts

### Platinum Tier
- Contribution: $50,000+ annually
- Maximum 8 named contacts, plus one executive sponsor

## Benefits
- **Bronze**: Logo on sponsors page, mention in monthly newsletter.
- **Silver**: Logo on README (below Gold/Platinum), 1 advisory survey response per quarter.
- **Gold**: Featured position on sponsors page (row 1), 2 advisory survey responses, ability to propose one non‑critical feature for consideration.
- **Platinum**: Top banner on website, dedicated blog post upon joining, one guaranteed feature request review per quarter.

## Requirements
- Each tier requires a signed agreement specific to that tier.
- Platinum sponsors must appoint a technical liaison to facilitate support requests.
- Gold+ sponsors must attend an annual compliance briefing (30 minutes, remote).

## Governance alignment
Tier definitions can be changed only by a 2/3 majority vote of the Core Team. Changes apply to new sponsors immediately and to renewing sponsors after a 90‑day notice period. No grandfathering of benefits without explicit consent.

## Branding rules
- Tier‑specific badges: “ApexOS Bronze Sponsor”, “ApexOS Silver Sponsor”, etc., with distinct color schemes (bronze, silver, gold, platinum hex codes defined in brand guide).
- Badges must not be altered (size, color, proportion).
- Platinum sponsors receive a physical plaque upon first payment.

## Public recognition rules
- Higher tiers always appear above lower tiers on all recognition surfaces.
- Within same tier, recognition order is randomized monthly to ensure fairness.
- Sponsors who upgrade mid‑year receive a press release template to announce the upgrade.
