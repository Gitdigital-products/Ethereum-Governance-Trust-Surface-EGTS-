Purpose

Defines the membership structure for the EGTS project, including rights and obligations.

Definitions

· Member: An individual or organization that has formally joined the EGTS community with certain privileges (e.g., voting on non‑technical matters).

Roles & Responsibilities

· GitDigital Products – Approves membership applications.
· RickCreator87 – Manages membership roster.

Requirements

· Membership requires acceptance of the project charter and code of conduct.
· Members may be removed for violations.

Membership Types

· Individual Member (non‑voting)
· Organizational Member (voting, limited to one vote per org)

Current Members

· GitDigital Products (Organizational)
· RickCreator87 (Individual)

Governance Alignment

Membership rights are defined in GOVERNANCE.md section 5.
