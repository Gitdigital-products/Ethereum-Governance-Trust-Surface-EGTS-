=== FILE: REVIEWERS.md ===
OWNER: GitDigital Products
AUTHOR: RickCreator87

Purpose

Specifies the expected reviewer pool for pull requests, review criteria, and the process for becoming a reviewer.

Definitions

· Reviewer: An individual who is authorized to approve pull requests but does not have merge rights (unless also a maintainer).

Roles & Responsibilities

· GitDigital Products – Appoints reviewers for critical paths.
· RickCreator87 – Trains reviewers on technical standards.
· Reviewers – Must respond to review requests within 2 business days.

Requirements

· Reviewers must have at least 5 accepted contributions to EGTS.
· A reviewer may be demoted by any owner for consistent low‑quality reviews.
· The default reviewer for any path is its code owner.

Current Reviewers

· GitDigital Products
· RickCreator87

Governance Alignment

Reviewer decisions are binding unless overridden by a code owner. This file works in tandem with CODEOWNERS and GOVERNANCE.md.
