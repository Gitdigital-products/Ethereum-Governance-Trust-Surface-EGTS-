# Sponsors

## Purpose
This document serves as the master registry and policy reference for all current and past Sponsors of the ApexOS project. It outlines how sponsors are listed, how to become a sponsor, and the collective recognition framework.

## Definitions
- **Active Sponsor**: A sponsor with a fully executed agreement and current payment within the last 12 months.
- **Past Sponsor**: A sponsor whose agreement has expired or been terminated more than 30 days ago.
- **Sponsor Wall**: The publicly accessible webpage listing all active sponsors with their tier and logo.

## Tiers (if applicable)
Same as defined in `sponsor.md`: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum.

## Benefits
- Collective visibility to a community of 50,000+ developers.
- Inclusion in quarterly impact reports distributed to all stakeholders.
- Invitation to annual sponsor‑only webinar with project leads.
- Right to display “Supported by ApexOS” on sponsor’s own marketing materials.

## Requirements
- Maintain active sponsorship status (no lapses exceeding 30 days).
- Designate a primary contact for sponsorship communications.
- Submit a high‑resolution logo (SVG or PNG, min 200x200px) within 14 days of signing.

## Governance alignment
Sponsors as a group have no formal governance role. The Core Team may create a Sponsors Advisory Group (non‑voting) to gather feedback on non‑technical matters. Any changes to sponsorship benefits must be approved by the Core Team.

## Branding rules
- The combined term “ApexOS Sponsors” may be used only by the project itself.
- Sponsors may not sublicense or redistribute ApexOS trademarks.
- Any co‑branded merchandise must be pre‑approved by the Core Team.

## Public recognition rules
- Sponsors page is updated weekly (every Friday UTC).
- Sponsors are listed in descending tier order; within same tier, by earliest join date.
- Past sponsors are moved to a separate “Alumni” section for one year.
- Social media shout‑outs: one per quarter for Gold+, one per year for Silver, none for Bronze unless special occasion.

## Templates (if applicable)
- Sponsor onboarding checklist: https://apexos.org/sponsors/onboarding
- Logo submission template: downloadable ZIP containing guidelines
