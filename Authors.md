Purpose

Extended documentation about authorship, including how to be listed, rights granted, and the CLA process.

Definitions

· Author: As defined in AUTHORS.
· Contributor License Agreement (CLA): Legal agreement granting the project rights to the contribution.

Roles & Responsibilities

· GitDigital Products – Hosts the CLA signing system.
· RickCreator87 – Assists contributors in completing the CLA.
· Contributors – Must sign the CLA before their name is added to AUTHORS.

Requirements

· An author entry must include full name and primary email.
· Corporate contributions list the organization as author unless an individual is specified.
· Removal of an author requires legal review and owner approval.

Template for author addition

```
Name: <full name>
Email: <email>
Organization (if any): <org>
Date of first contribution: YYYY-MM-DD
```

Governance Alignment

Ensures compliance with the project’s intellectual property policy, referenced in GOVERNANCE.md.
