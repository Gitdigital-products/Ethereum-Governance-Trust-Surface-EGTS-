# Supportship

## Purpose
This document defines the concept of “supportship” — the ongoing relationship between ApexOS and its recurring donors, distinct from sponsorship. It covers the philosophy, benefits, and community expectations.

## Definitions
- **Supportship**: The state of being a recurring supporter. It emphasizes long‑term, low‑barrier contribution.
- **Supportship Tenure**: The continuous length of time (in months) a supporter has maintained an active subscription.
- **Supportship Milestones**: Recognized at 6, 12, 24, and 36 months.

## Tiers (if applicable)
Supportship uses the same three tiers as `support_tier.md`: Bronze, Silver, Gold.

## Benefits
- All supporters receive a digital “Supportship Certificate” after 3 months.
- Silver+ supporters may nominate a feature request for community vote once per quarter.
- Gold supporters with 12+ months tenure receive a free ticket to ApexOS annual conference.

## Requirements
- Supportship requires a valid payment method on file.
- Supporters must keep their profile public on the donation platform (GitHub Sponsors / Open Collective) to receive recognition.
- Supportship can be paused for up to 30 days without losing tenure; after 30 days, tenure resets.

## Governance alignment
The Core Team may adjust supportship benefits at any time, with advance notice of 30 days via email to all active supporters. Supportship does not entitle supporters to any project decision‑making authority.

## Branding rules
- The term “ApexOS Supportship” is a trademark of the project and may only be used in official communications.
- Supporters may state “I support ApexOS” but not “ApexOS supports me” or similar.
- No co‑branding is permitted.

## Public recognition rules
- Supportship milestone announcements are made on the project’s social media (opt‑in by supporter).
- A “Hall of Supportship” page lists all supporters who have reached 24 months of consecutive support.
- Supporters who cancel are removed from active listings but remain in historical archives.

## Templates (if applicable)
- Supportship milestone email templates: `milestone_6m.md`, `milestone_12m.md`, etc., in `/templates/supportship/`
- Certificate generation script reference: https://apexos.org/supportship/certificate
