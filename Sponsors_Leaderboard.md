# Sponsors Leaderboard

## Purpose
This document defines the “Sponsors Leaderboard” — a ranking of organizational sponsors based on total cumulative financial contribution to the ApexOS project, including one‑time and recurring sponsorships.

## Definitions
- **Sponsors Leaderboard**: Public ranking of sponsor organizations by total amount contributed since project inception.
- **Lifetime Contribution**: Sum of all sponsorship payments, including past expired sponsorships.
- **Active vs. Inactive**: Active sponsors (current) are highlighted with a green dot; inactive with gray.

## Tiers (if applicable)
Not applicable; leaderboard includes all sponsors regardless of tier, but tier is displayed next to the organization name.

## Benefits
- Top 5 sponsors receive a “Platinum Plus” custom badge on the website.
- #1 sponsor gets a dedicated “Presented by” mention on the project’s homepage for one quarter.
- All sponsors on the leaderboard are invited to an annual “Leaders Dinner” (virtual or in‑person).

## Requirements
- Sponsor must have contributed at least $10,000 lifetime to appear.
- Contributions from parent companies and subsidiaries are aggregated if disclosed.
- Sponsors may request to be excluded from the leaderboard (opt‑out).

## Governance alignment
The leaderboard is managed by the Finance Subcommittee. Any dispute about contribution aggregation is resolved by referencing signed agreements. The leaderboard data is audited annually by an independent volunteer.

## Branding rules
- Leaderboard uses a monospace font for contribution amounts.
- Sponsor logos are not displayed on the leaderboard (only text names) to keep ranking clean.
- The leaderboard page must include a disclaimer: “Ranking is based on cumulative payments and does not imply product endorsement.”

## Public recognition rules
- Leaderboard is updated monthly on the 1st.
- A “Leaderboard Milestone” announcement is made whenever a sponsor crosses $50k, $100k, $500k.
- Past #1 sponsors are listed in a “Hall of Fame” section.

## Templates (if applicable)
- Contribution aggregation form for corporate groups: `/templates/aggregation_request.md`
- Leaderboard CSV export: `/exports/sponsors_leaderboard.csv`
