Purpose

Defines the role and obligations of a project sponsor for the EGTS project.

Definitions

· Sponsor: An organization or individual that provides funding, resources, or strategic support.

Roles & Responsibilities

· Sponsor:
  · Contributes according to the selected sponsorship tier.
  · Abides by the governance rules.
· GitDigital Products – Manages sponsor relationships.
· RickCreator87 – Ensures sponsor recognition is accurate.

Requirements

· Sponsors must sign a sponsorship agreement.
· Sponsorship does not grant voting rights beyond what is defined in GOVERNANCE.md.
· Sponsors may request a confidential status review.

Governance Alignment

Sponsorship terms are subordinate to the project charter and GOVERNANCE.md.
