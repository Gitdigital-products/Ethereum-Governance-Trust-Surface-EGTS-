OWNER: GitDigital Products
AUTHOR: RickCreator87

Purpose

Human‑readable documentation of maintainer roles, onboarding process, and expectations.

Definitions

· Maintainer: As defined in MAINTAINERS file.
· Emeritus Maintainer: A former maintainer without active merge rights but with advisory capacity.

Roles & Responsibilities

· Active Maintainer:
  · Perform code reviews.
  · Merge approved pull requests.
  · Update status.md and project_status.md after each release.
· GitDigital Products – Approve new maintainers.
· RickCreator87 – Train new maintainers on tooling and governance.

Requirements

· Maintainers must be listed in both MAINTAINERS and AUTHORS.md.
· A maintainer must pass a practical test (reviewing a sample PR).
· Removal requires majority vote of owners.

Template for maintainer nomination

```
Nominee: <GitHub handle>
Nominated by: <owner>
Area: <code/docs/infra>
Justification: <contributions>
```

Governance Alignment

Follows the governance‑as‑code principles of EGTS. All maintainer decisions are recorded in the repository log.
