# Sponsorship

## Purpose
This document defines the comprehensive sponsorship program of the ApexOS project, including eligibility, duration, termination, and the overall framework for all sponsorship‑related activities.

## Definitions
- **Sponsorship Program**: The entire set of tiers, benefits, rules, and agreements administered by the ApexOS Core Team.
- **Sponsorship Revenue**: All funds received through the program, net of transaction fees.
- **Fiscal Host**: The legal entity (e.g., Open Source Collective) that holds sponsorship funds on behalf of ApexOS.

## Tiers (if applicable)
| Tier     | Annual Contribution | Max # of sponsors |
|----------|--------------------|-------------------|
| Bronze   | $1k – $4.9k        | Unlimited         |
| Silver   | $5k – $14.9k       | 20                |
| Gold     | $15k – $49.9k      | 10                |
| Platinum | $50k+              | 5                 |

## Benefits
- All benefits per tier as defined in `sponsor.md`.
- Additionally, all sponsors receive a listing in the `SPONSORS.md` file in the project repository.
- Platinum sponsors receive a quarterly 1‑hour roadmap review with the Core Team.

## Requirements
- Sponsorship is open to any legal entity or individual not listed on any sanctions list.
- Sponsors must adhere to the ApexOS Privacy Policy and Data Processing Agreement.
- Sponsors must renew at least 30 days before expiration to avoid service interruption.

## Governance alignment
Sponsorship is a non‑equity, non‑voting relationship. The Core Team may terminate sponsorship immediately if a sponsor violates the Code of Conduct or uses project trademarks improperly. All sponsorship decisions are recorded in the public governance log.

## Branding rules
- Sponsors may not create derivative works of the ApexOS logo.
- The phrase “ApexOS Sponsor” may only be used as a factual statement, not as an endorsement of the sponsor’s products.
- Sponsors must remove all ApexOS branding within 15 days of sponsorship termination.

## Public recognition rules
- Recognition is automatically included in every `git shortlog` release announcement (sponsors list appended).
- Annual “State of ApexOS” report includes a thank‑you section with sponsor names.
- Sponsors who request anonymity are listed as “Anonymous Sponsor” with tier indicated.

## Templates (if applicable)
- Sponsorship renewal letter: https://apexos.org/sponsorship/renewal
- Sponsorship termination notice template: https://apexos.org/sponsorship/termination
