# Contribution Wall

## Purpose
This document defines the “Contribution Wall” — a recognition page for all types of contributions to ApexOS, including code, documentation, design, and financial contributions. It emphasizes holistic contribution.

## Definitions
- **Contribution Wall**: A webpage that lists contributors based on cumulative contribution points (not just money).
- **Contribution Point**: One point per dollar donated, one point per accepted pull request, ten points per hour of volunteer design work (self‑reported).
- **Wall Period**: Rolling 12 months.

## Tiers (if applicable)
- Bronze Contributor: 1 – 99 points
- Silver Contributor: 100 – 499 points
- Gold Contributor: 500 – 1999 points
- Platinum Contributor: 2000+ points

## Benefits
- Bronze: Name on wall, contributor role on Discord.
- Silver: All Bronze + priority for swag giveaways.
- Gold: All Silver + invitation to annual contributor summit.
- Platinum: All Gold + dedicated thank‑you blog post, custom ApexOS hoodie.

## Requirements
- Points are self‑reported via a form and verified by maintainers.
- Financial contributions are automatically counted via API.
- Code contributions are counted via GitHub API (merged PRs only).
- Abuse of point system leads to permanent removal from wall.

## Governance alignment
The Contribution Wall is maintained by the Governance Committee. Any change to point valuation requires a 2/3 majority vote. The wall’s purpose is to encourage diverse contributions, not to create hierarchy.

## Branding rules
- The wall uses the ApexOS brand colors but adds a “heart” icon for non‑financial contributions.
- No commercial advertising on the wall.
- Contributors may not use the wall screenshot for commercial purposes without permission.

## Public recognition rules
- The wall is updated daily at 00:00 UTC.
- Top 10 contributors each month are highlighted in the newsletter.
- Contributors who reach 5000 lifetime points receive a “Legend” status.

## Templates (if applicable)
- Contribution point claim form: https://apexos.org/contribution-wall/claim
- Points verification checklist: `/docs/points_verification.md`
