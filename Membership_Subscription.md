# Membership Subscription

## Purpose
This document defines the membership subscription model for ApexOS, which provides recurring financial support with additional community privileges distinct from sponsorship or support.

## Definitions
- **Member**: An individual who holds an active membership subscription to ApexOS.
- **Membership Cycle**: Monthly or annual billing, with benefits active as long as payment is current.
- **Member‑only Content**: Private forums, early access to certain project updates, and voting rights in non‑binding polls.

## Tiers (if applicable)
- Basic Member: $10/month or $100/year
- Pro Member: $25/month or $250/year
- Enterprise Member: $100/month or $1000/year (for organizations, up to 5 seats)

## Benefits
- Basic: Member badge on Discord, access to `#members` channel, voting in community polls.
- Pro: All Basic + monthly 1‑hour “Ask the Core Team” session, 20% discount on merchandise.
- Enterprise: All Pro + dedicated account manager, ability to host an ApexOS meetup under project brand, logo on membership page.

## Requirements
- Members must be at least 18 years old or have parental consent.
- Members must abide by the Member Code of Conduct (stricter than general CoC regarding respectful discourse in member areas).
- Enterprise members must designate a primary contact for billing.

## Governance alignment
Membership fees are considered unrestricted funds. Members may participate in advisory polls but have no binding vote on technical decisions. The Core Team may create a Member Council (elected annually from Pro and Enterprise members) to advise on community matters.

## Branding rules
- Members may use “ApexOS Member” badge, with tier indicated (Basic/Pro/Enterprise).
- Badge must link to https://apexos.org/membership.
- Enterprise members may include the ApexOS logo on their internal employee portal (only for employee recognition, not external marketing).

## Public recognition rules
- A “Member Wall” lists all active members by tier (first name + last initial, opt‑out available).
- Pro and Enterprise members are invited to an annual virtual gala.
- Members who maintain subscription for 24 consecutive months receive a “Charter Member” title.
