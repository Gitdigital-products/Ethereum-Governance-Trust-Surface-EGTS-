Purpose

Recognizes all individuals who have contributed to the EGTS project, including non‑code contributions (e.g., documentation, testing, governance feedback).

Definitions

· Contributor: Any person who has submitted a pull request, opened an issue, or provided significant non‑code assistance.

Roles & Responsibilities

· GitDigital Products – Maintains the contributors list.
· RickCreator87 – Reviews and approves additions to the list.
· Contributors – Abide by the code of conduct and governance rules.

Requirements

· Contributors are added by self‑nomination or owner nomination.
· Each contributor must have a GitHub account linked to an email.
· The list is updated quarterly.

Contributor Recognition

· All contributors are thanked in release notes.
· Top contributors (by impact) are mentioned in sponsor_wall.md.
