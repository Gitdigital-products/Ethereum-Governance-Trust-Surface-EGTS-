# Support

## Purpose
This document defines the support offerings provided to individuals and organizations that financially support ApexOS, distinct from sponsorship. “Support” refers to recurring contributions via platforms like GitHub Sponsors or Patreon at lower amounts.

## Definitions
- **Supporter**: An individual or organization contributing a recurring monthly amount between $5 and $999 (or one‑time annual equivalent).
- **Support Tier**: Bronze Supporter ($5‑$49/mo), Silver Supporter ($50‑$199/mo), Gold Supporter ($200‑$999/mo).
- **Enterprise Support**: Custom support agreements above $1,000/mo (handled separately).

## Tiers (if applicable)
- Bronze Supporter
- Silver Supporter
- Gold Supporter

## Benefits
- Bronze: Name on supporters page, access to supporter‑only Discord role.
- Silver: All Bronze benefits + monthly Q&A webinar with maintainers.
- Gold: All Silver benefits + priority issue labeling (one issue per month flagged “supporter-priority”), 10% discount on ApexOS merchandise.

## Requirements
- Supporters must have an active subscription for at least 3 months to receive benefits.
- Supporters may cancel anytime; benefits cease immediately upon cancellation.
- No formal agreement required; terms of service on the donation platform apply.

## Governance alignment
Supporters have no governance authority. The Core Team may occasionally poll supporters for non‑binding feedback on features. Support contributions are non‑refundable.

## Branding rules
- Supporters may use “ApexOS Supporter” badge but not “Sponsor”.
- Badge must be displayed at no more than 100x100 pixels.
- Supporters may not use the ApexOS logo in any commercial advertising.

## Public recognition rules
- Supporters are listed on `/supporters` page, grouped by tier.
- One random supporter is thanked in each commit message template (automated via bot).
- Supporters who maintain a subscription for 12 consecutive months receive a “Founding Supporter” designation.

## Templates (if applicable)
- Support onboarding email (sent automatically by payment processor)
- Support cancellation survey: https://apexos.org/support/cancel-survey
