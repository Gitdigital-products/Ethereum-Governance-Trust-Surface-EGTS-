=== FILE: supporting.md ===

OWNER: GitDigital Products

AUTHOR: RickCreator87

Purpose

Guide for individuals and organizations that wish to support EGTS without becoming a formal sponsor.

Definitions

· Supporting: Contributing through non‑financial means (e.g., code, documentation, community management).

Roles & Responsibilities

· GitDigital Products – Provides guidelines.
· RickCreator87 – Reviews supporting contributions.

Requirements

· Supporting activities must be aligned with project goals.
· Significant support may lead to invitation for sponsorship.

Ways to Support

· Submit code or documentation pull requests.
· Triage GitHub issues.
· Translate documentation.
· Provide legal or security advice.

Governance Alignment

Supporting is recognized in CONTRIBUTORS.md and may be elevated by owners.
