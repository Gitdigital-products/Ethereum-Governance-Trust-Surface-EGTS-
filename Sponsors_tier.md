# Sponsors Tier

## Purpose
This document defines how multiple sponsors are grouped and displayed according to their sponsorship tier, including capacity limits and waitlist management.
w2
## Definitions
- **Sponsors Tier Group**: All active sponsors belonging to the same contribution tier.
- **Tier Capacity**: Maximum number of sponsors allowed in a given tier (if any).
- **Waitlist**: Ordered list of prospective sponsors for a tier that is currently full.

## Tiers (if applicable)
- Bronze: No capacity limit.
- Silver: Capacity 20.
- Gold: Capacity 10.
- Platinum: Capacity 5.

## Benefits
- Sponsors in a tier share collective benefits such as joint invitations to sponsor meetups.
- When capacity is reached, new applicants are placed on a waitlist and notified when a slot opens.
- Tier groups receive a dedicated channel on the sponsor Discord server.

## Requirements
- To join a tier with capacity, a sponsor must either wait for an open slot or outbid an existing sponsor at renewal (Core Team mediates).
- Sponsors may not transfer their slot to another entity.
- Waitlisted entities are not charged until they enter the tier.

## Governance alignment
The Core Team reserves the right to increase tier capacity after community consultation. Any dispute regarding tier assignment or waitlist order is resolved by the Governance Committee. All waitlist decisions are logged publicly.

## Branding rules
- Sponsors in the same tier are displayed alphabetically (except Platinum, which is by join date).
- No sponsor may claim a “higher” tier than their actual contribution.
- Tier grouping must be visually distinct on the sponsors page (horizontal separators).

## Public recognition rules
- Each tier group is recognized as a whole during annual report (e.g., “Our Gold Sponsors: ...”).
- Waitlisted entities are not publicly acknowledged until they become active sponsors.
- Full tiers are indicated with a “(full)” label next to the tier name on the website.

## Templates (if applicable)
- Waitlist confirmation email: plain text template stored in repository under `/templates/waitlist_email.txt`
- Tier slot release announcement: Markdown template for blog posts
