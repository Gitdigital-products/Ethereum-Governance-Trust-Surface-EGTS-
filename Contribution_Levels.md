OWNER: GitDigital Products

AUTHOR: RickCreator87

Purpose

Defines levels of contribution (e.g., Beginner, Regular, Lead) and the criteria to achieve each.

Definitions

· Contribution Level: A badge reflecting engagement.

Roles & Responsibilities

· GitDigital Products – Defines levels.
· RickCreator87 – Assigns levels.

Requirements

· Levels are non‑binding and for recognition only.

Level Criteria
Beginner First merged pull request
Regular 10+ merged PRs or 20+ issues triaged
Lead 50+ merged PRs or maintainer role

Governance Alignment

Levels may be referenced in sponsorship benefits but carry no formal authority.
